Basic Type of Creature

Luna

Luna are evolved creatures created through mana mutation, fusion, or magical experiments.

  • Powerful and adaptable
  • Unique abilities
  • Unstable mana
  • Shorter lifespan

Elio

Elio are the original creatures of Ophelia, born naturally from the Guardian Beasts and elemental mana.

  • Natural mana users
  • Long lifespan
  • Stable evolution
  • Protect the balance of nature

The Lore of Ophelia Fantasy World

Long ago, the world of Ophelia was shaped by the power of the Five Elements. Each kingdom was protected by a Guardian Beast that maintained the balance of nature and mana. As civilizations flourished, mysterious Luna creatures began to emerge through corrupted and artificial mana, threatening the harmony of the world. Now, adventurers must journey across the five elemental regions, uncover ancient secrets, and decide the future of Ophelia.
